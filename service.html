Service

Central Texas Tennis Association Volunteer Coach

Becoming a volunteer coach was my way of returning the favor to my past mentors that helped me improve at tennis. While working with other coaches, I mentored young children on the basics of the game and managed 3-hour long programs, consisting of drills, stretches, and games.

Summer Youth Public Library Program

Working as a youth volunteer in a local library, I shelved books, reorganized media, and helped visitors to find what they were looking for. Additionally, if the opportunity arose, I educated children about the Dewey Decimal classification system.
